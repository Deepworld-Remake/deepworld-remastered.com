Welcome to Deepworld Remastered


I guess I should introduce myself as the writer here... Im Taylor Schneider. While online, I go by Mister SirCode. Ive been developing a remake, remodel and ultimately a remastering of Deepworld, a Massively Multiplayer MMORPG originally created by Bytebin Studios.


I wanted to give the game new life. I myself took part in the "modding" and "cheating" that ultimately shook the game to its core. It was shutdown at the end of August, 2019. I thought it only fair that I myself rebuild something that I helped destroy.


Im currently working on the game every day, along with these websites, an API, a codex and a Discord bot named "Arthur" after one of the ingame NPC androids.


If you wish to take part in the journey of this game's development, feel free to hop on our Development Server below




You can get the game on Gamejolt where most versions and updates are.. Plus a custom version with Autoupdates that works for the Gamejolt Client




Or you can download it on itch.io
